About Us

The ISAT-U Registrar's Online Transaction and E-Payment System is a digital platform designed to streamline and enhance the efficiency of academic transactions within the context of the Iloilo Science and Technology University (ISAT-U). This system enables students, faculty, and administrative staff to perform various academic and financial transactions through a user-friendly online interface.

Learn More

Services

Online Registration Services

  • Streamlining the process of course registration and enrollment.

  • Allowing students to update personal and academic information electronically.

  • Providing a user-friendly interface for efficient registration workflows.

E-Payment Services

  • Facilitating electronic payments for tuition fees and other financial transactions.

  • Ensuring secure and transparent financial transactions.

  • Providing digital receipts and tracking of payment history.

Transaction Tracking Services

  • Offering real-time status updates on registration and payment processes.

  • Sending notifications for important academic deadlines.

  • Allowing users to track the progress of their transactions within the system.
